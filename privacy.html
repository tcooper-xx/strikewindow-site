Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

StrikeWindow is a solunar activity and weather application for fishing and hunting. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Location Data

  • What: GPS coordinates or manually entered location
  • Why: To calculate solunar activity scores, weather forecasts, and sunrise/sunset times specific to your location
  • Storage: Saved locally on your device only — never transmitted to our servers

Usage Data (Firebase)

  • What: App feature flag configuration fetched from Firebase Remote Config
  • Why: To remotely configure app features without requiring an update
  • Firebase may collect anonymous diagnostic data per Google's Privacy Policy
  • No personally identifiable information is sent to Firebase

Weather Data

  • What: Your coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to retrieve forecasts
  • Why: To display weather conditions for your location
  • Open-Meteo is a free, open-source API that does not require account creation. See Open-Meteo's privacy policy

Map & Geocoding Data

  • What: Your coordinates are used to fetch map tiles and reverse geocode locations
  • Why: To display maps and convert coordinates to place names
  • Provider: OpenStreetMap / Nominatim. See OpenStreetMap's privacy policy

On-Device AI Android only

  • Solunar and weather data is processed locally by Gemini Nano (Google AI Core)
  • This generates a plain-language daily activity summary
  • This processing happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to external AI servers by StrikeWindow.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

Data Storage

All app data (saved locations, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data.

StrikeWindow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
